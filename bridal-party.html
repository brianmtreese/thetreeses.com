On Brian’s Side

(Best Man)

A truly “unique” individual who has managed to keep me on my toes for over fifteen years now. (Best Man)

I’m honored that he will be up there with me on the big day.

Jake Wackerman

(Best Man)

If ever I’ve found myself down in the dumps he’s been the one to call. Jake is the life of every party and can swing dance with the best of them (ladies beware).
